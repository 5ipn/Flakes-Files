The Robux Theft Era

Everyone in SJC thought they already knew how messy Flakes was, but October and December proved they didn’t even know half of it. Flakes, also known as James, the 22-year-old almost-23 femboy menace of Donation Nation, wasn’t just manipulating people or hosting fake giveaways. He was running one of the biggest Robux theft operations the community had ever seen.

And he did all of this under the same name: Flakes.
Like he wanted people to remember who scammed them.

Stealing Robux in Donation Nation

James didn’t grind for anything. His entire Donation Nation “success” was built on tricking, lying, stealing, and manipulating anyone dumb enough to believe him.

He used every scam possible:

  • Pretending he would double players’ donations
  • Acting like he was a mod inspecting booths
  • Faking glitches to get access to someone’s stand
  • Telling people he was sponsored by big creators
  • Offering fake shoutouts for Robux
  • Pretending he was running SJC “tax events”

People saw him do it constantly.
Noom, Lexie, Zonez, Val, and CXI all witnessed him scam kids for hours straight.
Staroflight caught him telling some little kid he needed “temporary booth access” and then taking all the Robux and vanishing.
Seppgen said he once watched Flakes pretend to cry in voice chat so people would feel bad and donate.

James didn’t even hide it well.
He just did it fast.

Using stolen funds to boost his stats

One of the biggest jokes in the community was how James bragged about his raised and donated numbers like he actually earned them. But everyone found out the truth.

He was literally funneling stolen Robux into his own alts, then donating to himself to make it look like he was some elite top-tier player.

Jens and Noah watched him switch between accounts so fast that he confused his own usernames.
Tripl3tsgamer said Flakes bragged about being in the “top donor circle” when all he did was steal from kids.

Every number he flexed was fake.
Every donation he made was washed through alts.
Every “achievement” he showed off was bought with someone else’s money.

Stealing Christmas presents

December was when people realized James wasn’t just a scammer. He was a villain in a Hallmark movie but without the character development.

And then Ronaldo told everyone he heard James brag about “holiday hunting.”
Nobody knew what that meant until Leanne saw him pocketing Christmas gifts that were meant for new players.
Bugz literally called him the “Grinch of SJC,” and the name stuck instantly.

James didn’t only steal Robux.
He stole people’s Christmas.

Who wakes up in the morning and says, “Yeah, I’m gonna ruin someone’s holiday today”?
James did.
