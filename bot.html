FLAKES FILES - PART 01

The Origin of Flakes, aka James

Before all the drama, before the scams, before the furry allegations, most people in the SJC community only knew him as Flakes. Hardly anyone realized his real name was James, a 22-year-old who always mentioned he was turning 23 soon like it was some badge of wisdom. He acted like being older meant he was more mature, but honestly he behaved like the opposite.

He first showed up in SJC randomly one evening, drifting into Donation Nation wearing pastel outfits and a whole femboy aesthetic. Cat ears. Tiny skirts. Boots that didn’t match anything. People like Noom, Staroflight, and Itz_dev were the first to talk to him, and even they later said something about him just felt off from the start.

James talked in this overly soft uwu tone that didn’t match his age at all. He called everyone “hun” or “cutie” and posed like he was constantly taking selfies. It was like he reinvented himself every time he logged in.

The Free Robux Con

James built his entire personality around promising free Robux. He acted like he was secretly rich or had some dev connection. Every time someone walked past his booth, he said he could give them a huge donation but needed “a little help” reaching some goal first.

People believed him way too easily.
ZylozVA donated first and got absolutely nothing back.

Then Tripl3tsgamer, Lexie, Zonez, Val, and even KindlyVernon all fell for the exact same routine.

James always had excuses.
“My pending is still loading.”
“Roblox is checking my account.”
“I swear I’ll donate later, I’m just lagging.”

It was the same lines every time, and somehow they kept working. People started making memes about the “Flakes Pending Curse,” but the donations still came in. New players didn’t know, and James took full advantage.

The Friend Recycling Problem

Something everyone eventually noticed was how James treated friends like temporary items. He would act super close to you when you donated. He’d spam you with heart emojis, call you his favorite person, and talk to you every day.

Then once you stopped giving him anything, he dropped you instantly.

Seppgen, cxi, Jens, noah, and Chloe all went through the exact same cycle. They donated, became his “best friend,” then after two or three weeks of not giving him more, James blocked them or ghosted them forever.

He didn’t care about people. He cared about what they could give him. Once someone’s wallet dried up, their friendship with James was basically over.

The Furry Rumors Explode

The furry rumors started when Morse caught James wearing a glowing wolf avatar in the middle of a random server at 2 a.m. Nobody thought much of it until Ronaldo said he heard James say weird animal roleplay lines in VC.

Then Leanne mentioned she once saw him wearing a fluffy tail and paw gloves and that he acted like it was totally normal.

When people asked James directly if he was a furry, he would just giggle and say “maybeee” with like five e’s. He never denied it. He never admitted it. He just kept the rumor alive on purpose.

Even Bugz, who barely gets involved in drama, said that out of all the accusations about James, the furry one was probably the most believable.

The Flakes Federation Disaster

Things really went crazy when James decided he deserved power in the SJC community. He started forming this weird group he called the Flakes Federation. He tried convincing new players that SJC leadership was “corrupt” and that he would fix everything if they supported him.

He targeted people he thought were easy to influence.
Noom said James messaged him nonstop asking him to help with his uprising.
Staroflight said James promised her admin if she sided with him.
Itz_dev leaked that James made an entire document explaining why he should control SJC, and the whole thing was written in pink sparkly text like a Valentine’s Day card.

He tried starting drama with Zonez, Val, and Lexie, claiming they were sabotaging him. None of it was true. He just wanted attention and control.

The "takeover" lasted like five days before everyone realized he was delusional.

The Legacy of James

Now James is basically a myth within SJC. New players hear stories about him before they even meet him. Everyone from Noom to Bugz has a story about being scammed, lied to, or dragged into drama because of him.

He still shows up sometimes, usually on alt accounts, pretending to be someone completely new. But the second he says anything about free Robux, people instantly know it’s him.

James being 22 going on 23 only makes it more ridiculous. He’s a grown adult scamming teens on Roblox for virtual currency while wearing cat ears and possibly being a furry.

He might come back again with another scheme or a new fake personality, but the SJC community is ready for him now. Nobody wants to live through another James era.

And honestly, nobody ever wants to see the Flakes Federation rise again.

Safety note: please avoid this person and their devious activites on DISCORD and SJC at all costs.
